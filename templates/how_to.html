Verbum Disco: How to play


  1. Upload the .txt file with words and their translations.
    The word and translated word in .txt file have to be in a one line separated by semi-column and 1 space. Each 2-tuple of words have to be in new line. At least 5 2-tuples of words should be added.

  2. Click the 'SUBMIT' button.
    The set of the uploaded words will be stored.

  3. Click the 'PLAY' button to start the game.

  4. Try to translate {{ no_of_selected}} randomly chosen words which will be displayed.

  5. After submitting your input, you will see the results and the points gained.

  6. You can continue playing or end the game.

To start the game, click above on 'Verbum Disco game'.
